ABOUT NITYOHUM

NITYOHUM is a not-for-profit organization in India, registered under the Indian Trust Act of 1882. Set up in 28 August 2019, the organization works for the health and happiness of disadvantaged people of society especially children and older persons to improve their quality of life.

NITYOHUM is working towards needy children to meet their dreams. We as an organization believe that children shouldn’t be denied their rights because of where they were born. Our volunteers are working day and night to spread smile on the faces of the unprivileged children. Our team is also providing them with healthy & hygienic food of their choice and also making arrangements for studies & sports resources.

NITYOHUM envisions a society where elderly have the right to an active, healthy and dignified life. We extend a helping hand to senior citizens wherever required. We are providing emergency eldercare services to the elderly ones in urban as well as rural areas, with all essential resources such as high-quality food cooked, prescribed medicines, a wide range of information and counseling support.

NITYOHUM is dedicated to inner transformation, offering a variety of powerful tools for well-being to people from all walks of life. Our fundamental vision is to offer the science of inner wellbeing to every human being a science that helps a person realize the unlimited potential within. NITYOHUM is also working on an integrative therapy in the field of health care to provide a healthy life to common man. We want to combine modern healthcare system with the techniques of mindfulness & meditation to bring such an integral method of treatment, which will also make it possible to treat the cause of the disease. We are committed to holistic health-physical, mental, emotional & spiritual wellbeing of humanity.

All donations to NITYOHUM are eligible for tax exemption under section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

MISSION

Providing healthy and happy life to common people is the main mission of NITYOHUM. Food and Education for the needy children, Tailoring training center to make girls self-reliant, Old age home, Handicapped, Orphan services, Flood relief, Annadan and Gau sewa is our regular practice. We are also working for water, soil, climate and forest conservation, plastic free cosmos, pesticide free agricultural and sanitation as responsibility to the nation and nature. We are in service of the human being throughout the country mainly in areas of UP, Bihar, MP, Jharkhand, Uttarakhand, Delhi, Orissa, maharastra and karnatka. We want to expand the same in maximum underprivileged remote areas.

VISION

NITYOHUM is continuously working for oneness and wellness through scientifically proven deep and ancient unification techniques. Achieving state of non duality is our ultimate goal by process of purification of body, mind and soul. The feeling of joy and satisfaction along with total happiness and health is a natural phenomenon, but these feelings are not experienced by people for long. Many people suffer from stress in the competitive world. Human being is living every moment in fear of losing something. A human being can only fulfill superficial happiness with enough money or material means, but overall happiness is a state of mind, heart and soul. You need to take care of your body to be happy, your whole being is in you. The purpose of NITYOHUM is to infuse confidence in you by connecting you with your infinite powers.
